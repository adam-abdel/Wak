Makroud El-Kairouan

6 DT

Discover the Sweet Taste of Kairouan! Indulge in the authentic Makroud El-Kairouan — a traditional Tunisian pastry made with fine semolina, pure dates, and a touch of natural spices, then fried and dipped in rich honey. Handcrafted with care, this iconic sweet brings you the true flavor of Kairouan in every bite.

The Tunisian Beskout

5 DT

Soft, Sweet & Unforgettable – The Tunisian Beskout Light, crumbly, and gently sweet, Beskout (Tunisian finger biscuits) are a nostalgic favorite across generations. Perfect with tea or coffee, these handcrafted biscuits melt in your mouth and bring the comforting taste of home with every bite.

WAKALNI-Sweet & Local

Discover the rich world of Tunisian sweets! Tunisian desserts are a true celebration of tradition and flavor — from the iconic Makroud of Kairouan, to almond-filled Baklawa , rose-scented Zrir , and melt-in-your-mouth Kaak Warka . Handmade with love and natural ingredients, our sweets reflect generations of artisanal know-how and bring the taste of Tunisia right to your table.
